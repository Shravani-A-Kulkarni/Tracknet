About TrackNet

TrackNet leverages AI, Computer Vision, and IoT-enabled CCTV networks** to provide intelligent surveillance for smart cities and secure environments. Our robust system detects and analyzes events in real-time, drastically improving road safety, traffic management, and public monitoring capabilities. We are committed to building safer and smarter communities.

Founded in 2025, our mission is to empower law enforcement and city planners with cutting-edge technology that moves beyond simple recording. We aim to create proactive surveillance systems that predict and prevent incidents, making urban life safer and more efficient for everyone.
